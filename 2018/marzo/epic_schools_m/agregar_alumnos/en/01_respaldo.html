Creating Profiles for your Students

Hi! Here is a short video regarding how to add students in Yogome Epic Teachers. Enjoy!

Step by step:

  1. 1. Log in to the Yogome Epic Teachers App.
  2. 2. Select the group that you would like to add your students to.
  3. 3. Go to the lower left of the screen to the “Students” section.
  4. 4. Select “Create a New Student”.
  5. 5. Select your preferred character to identify the student.
  6. 6. Add the name (nickname) of the student.
  7. 7. ***Optional: Add the email address of the student’s parent or tutor.
  8. 8. Select "Save".
