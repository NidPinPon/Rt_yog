Instructions for your students to login

Hi! Here is a short video regarding how your students can login with Yogome Epic Schools. Enjoy!

Step by step:

  1. 1. Open the Epic Teachers App.
  2. 2. Select Enter.
    If you don’t have a username and a password yet, sign up, or contact us: help@yogome.com
  3. 3. Write your username.
  4. 4. Type your password.
  5. 5. Tap on Enter.
  6. 6. You’re all set! You can now enjoy being an Epic Teacher.
