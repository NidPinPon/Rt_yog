Consistency is the key

What’s the best way to help your child reinforce what they learn in school? Even though there is no perfect way to do it, science tells us that children learn better when there’s a full-immersion experience.

Yogome is an example of how to make learning an immersive experience for children. Below, there are some tips from parents worldwide, who use Yogome apps with their children.

Include them in the evening schedule

Use Yogome as a warm-up before your children do their homework (during the school year) or as a fun activity between any other chore during the afternoon.

Play together

Spend quality time with your children and discover their way of thinking. You can also help them if they have questions about any topic.

Get involved, make questions

Ask your children what they learned today or what exercises they liked the most. Can they relate it to what they are learning at school?

Make it short or long enough so they have fun

We recommend 20 minutes a day, but even a couple of games for 5 minutes are good enough as long as your kids are having fun.
