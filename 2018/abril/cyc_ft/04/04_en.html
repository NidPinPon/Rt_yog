Consistency is key

Parents wonder what is the best way to help their children reinforce the skills they learn at school. The truth is that there is no such thing as a perfect way. Children learn best when they are engaged and motivated with the experience.

Yogome is one company that is striving to make learning an engaging and enjoyable experience for children.

To build the best product for children, Yogome is using parent feedback to build their learning experience. The following are the top tips from parents around the around the world who are using the Yogome suite.

Make it a part of the afternoon schedule

Use it as a warm-up for your children before homework, as a fun break between chores, or practice during the afternoon.
